This project showcases how to make your waterproof servo actuated pan/tilt camera holder.
We designed this project for everyone, who wants to test outdoor camera capabilities mounted on mobile platforms (like for ex. Turtle Rover).

Rotation of camera holder is provided by standard sized D3015 waterproof servo. Force created by the weight of camera and second servo is working along the axis of rotation so additional bearing is not required â€“ metal servo horn with threads will make it.
